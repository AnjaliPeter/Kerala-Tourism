Kozhikode

"A colonial city of trade and tourism"

Formerly known as Calicut, Kozhikode is located in Kerala. It was the Capital of Malabar during the Zamorin rule 500 years ago and is famous for its centuries-old trade in cotton and spices with Jews, Arabs, Phoenicians and Chinese.Vasco da Gama had set his foot on the remote beach of Kappad in Kozhikode in 1498.
